    Assumptions / Disclaimer

    Important source and calculation notes.

    Important: This planner is an estimate based on the 2027 Tupper Manor wedding package PDF. Final pricing may vary based on contract terms, taxes, administrative fee treatment, F&B minimum interpretation, guest count, menu selections, bar consumption, market-price items, availability, vendor policies, gratuity, and date-specific terms. Always confirm the final all-in proposal directly with the venue before making a decision.

    • The PDF attachment was not available in this workspace, so the implementation uses the pricing and package details supplied in the request as the project data source.
    • Engagement gift discounts are marked “confirm with venue” because the special is stated as valid through June 1, 2026.
    • Spring Saturday is marked sold out with unavailable pricing in the prompt; this planner displays the Spring Friday/Sunday pricing as a reference while warning that the date is sold out.
